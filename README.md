# End-to-End-Supply-Chain-Analytics-Project-SQL-Power-BI-Gen-AI

Key Business Insights
🔹 A small number of products contributed a significant portion of total revenue, indicating strong product concentration.
🔹 Certain warehouses consistently processed more orders than others, revealing workload imbalance across locations.
🔹 Delayed shipments were concentrated in a few warehouses, highlighting opportunities to improve delivery performace.
🔹 Some suppliers generated higher sales and maintained stronger inventory availability, while others contributed relatively less.
🔹 A small group of customers accounted for a large share of revenue, emphasizing the importance of customer retention strategies.
🔹 Return analysis identified recurring return reasons, helping pinpoint areas for product quality and logistics improvement.
🔹 Inventory analysis revealed products operating close to or below reorder levels, increasing the risk of stockouts.
🔹 Monthly sales trends showed fluctuations that can support better demand forecasting and inventory planning.


Business Improvement Recommendations
📦 Rebalance inventory across warehouses to reduce stock shortages and improve order fulfillment.
🚚 Investigate the root causes of shipment delays in underperforming warehouses and optimize logistics operations.
📈 Focus marketing efforts on high-performing product categories while creating strategies to improve lower-performing ones.
🤝 Strengthen relationships with high-performing suppliers and review the performance of lower-performing suppliers.
📊 Implement automated inventory alerts to trigger replenishment before products fall below reorder levels.
🎯 Develop customer loyalty programs for high-value customers to increase retention and lifetime value.
📅 Use historical sales trends to improve demand forecasting and procurement planning.
📉 Analyze return patterns to reduce avoidable returns through better quality control and packaging improvements.
